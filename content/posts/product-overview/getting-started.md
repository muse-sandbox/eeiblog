The multiple styles of accompaniments also let students experience a wide variety of musical styles on each exercise. They can practice the same exercise multiple times, each time with a different accompaniment.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>3. EEi Online Instructional Videos</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image -->
<figure class="wp-block-image"><img src="https://i0.wp.com/images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5b928a1e75f9ee72cbaf8c6f/1539183287898-DOPP5TPSMKQQW3VH8VRT/image-asset.png?ssl=1" alt="Online instructional videos preview"/></figure>

<p>Many teachers love having these instructional videos to help their students understand instrument assembly and care, tone production, hand position and more. These are easy to use in class and can be used again at home as reinforcement.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p class="has-text-align-center"><strong><em>Since they are online, students can view these on any device.</em></strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>EEi Tech Tip:</strong> If you have devices in your class (iPads, Chromebooks, etc.), have each section do a group activity and watch their instrument video together.</p>

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>4. Make Class Recordings</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Make a quick recording of your students in class for them to hear. Kids always love hearing their group performances and the EEi Music Studio allows you to record on any internet-connected device. Save it online for a few months down the road to show students their progress throughout the year.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph {"align":"center"} -->
<p class="has-text-align-center"><strong><em>The recording feature in the EEi Music Studio is easy to use and stores all your recordings in the cloud.</em></strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
