# Essential Elements & EEi — abbreviations for content writing

Curated subset of internal Muse Group abbreviations, narrowed to acronyms likely to appear in EE / EEi customer-facing copy (blog posts, tutorials, marketing pages). Internal HR / finance / security acronyms are omitted. Full reference list lives in `source/Abbreviations-original.md`.

**Rule:** if an abbreviation appears in copy, spell it out at least once on first use, then the short form is fine.

## Brand & company

| Acronym | Full term | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| EE | Essential Elements | The Hal Leonard music education method series. Spell out on first use. |
| EEi | Essential Elements Interactive | The cloud-based companion to the EE method. Always lowercase "i" attached to "EE". |
| HL | Hal Leonard | Customer-facing copy: write **Hal Leonard** in full, do not use "HL" with readers. |
| HLE | Hal Leonard Europe | Internal — avoid in public copy. |
| HLUS | Hal Leonard US | Internal — avoid in public copy. |
| HLO | Hal Leonard Online (halleonard.com) | Use the URL or "Hal Leonard's website" with readers. |
| MG | Muse Group | The parent company of Hal Leonard. Spell out on first use. |

## EEi feature shorthand (capitalize as shown, no abbreviation needed)

These are feature names, not acronyms — write them out, never abbreviate:

- **Music Studio** — the EEi practice / recording / playback tool.
- **SoundCheck Performance Assessment** (full first reference) → **SoundCheck** afterwards.
- **MatchMySound** — the engine that powers SoundCheck. Mention with full spelling on first reference: "SoundCheck, powered by MatchMySound."
- **Teacher Audio Feedback** — the audio feedback feature.
- **Video Assignments** — the video recording assignment feature.
- **Recording Assignments** — audio + video assignment workflow.
- **Bonus Songs** — pop-tune library inside EEi.
- **Music Theory Worksheets** — downloadable PDF resources inside EEi.
- **Google Classroom Integration** — the SSO + assignment-sync feature.
